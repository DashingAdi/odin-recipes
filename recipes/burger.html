Burger Recipe

Description

A burger is a handheld sandwich made with a cooked ground meat patty, typically beef, placed between two buns. It is often garnished with various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, onions, and condiments like ketchup or mayonnaise. Burgers are a popular fast food item known for their delicious combination of flavors and versatility.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Begin by whisking the egg, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl. Then, add the ground beef and bread crumbs before forming the mixture into ¾-inch patties. Place the patties on a preheated grill and cook for six to eight minutes per side.
  2. Make sure the beef's temperature reads at least 160 degrees F before serving, and you're ready to go. Top with classic condiments like mustard, ketchup, and mayo for full-fledged flavor.
